Department of Computer Science

Department of Computer Science The Department of Computer Science is under the Faculty of Applied Sciences and has been training students in Higher National Diploma programme since 2007. Currently the department runs HND in Computer Science, Diploma in Information Technology, Diploma in Hardware and Networking, Diploma in Web Application and Database Management, and other certificate courses. The Department trains students in various fields of computing and equip them with knowledge for development and utilization of modern computing equipment thereby placing them in positions that are in high demand in society as well as research.
